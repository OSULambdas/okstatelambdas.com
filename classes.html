What's this all about?

Lambda Phi Epsilon is an Asian-interest fraternity founded in 1981 at the University of California, Los Angeles. It emphasizes leadership, academic excellence, cultural awareness, and brotherhood. With a focus on promoting Asian-American identity, Lambda Phi Epsilon has expanded to numerous universities and is known for its commitment to community service and fostering a strong sense of brotherhood among its members.
